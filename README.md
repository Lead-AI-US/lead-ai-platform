# Lead.AI Platform

Main AI automation platform for lead capture, customer engagement, and business workflow automation.

## Status

Planned

This repository is a public foundation for the future Lead.AI platform. It does not yet contain a runnable production application.

## Product Vision

Lead.AI Platform will help small businesses manage AI-powered conversations, lead capture, appointment workflows, and customer intelligence from one operational dashboard.

## Planned Capabilities

- Website and messaging lead capture.
- AI-assisted lead qualification.
- Customer conversation history.
- Appointment automation and reminders.
- Workflow rules for follow-up and handoff.
- Analytics for response time, lead quality, and conversion.

## Intended Users

- Small business owners.
- Sales and operations teams.
- Service businesses that rely on appointments, inquiries, and follow-up.

## Technology Direction

- React-based frontend.
- API layer for lead, conversation, and automation workflows.
- Database-backed organization and user model.
- AI orchestration with provider-specific integrations isolated behind clear service modules.
